NFL Week 5 Free Bracket

Contest Rules

1) One Entry per User

2) Most W wins Bracket

3) User’s make 16 picks + Tie Breaker in Confirmation screen

4) W are based off spread vs league results as reported in SportHitters App

5) If two or more users have same W amount, Tie-Breaker will be assessed. The user closest to *Tie Breaker Total will win Bracket

6) Once picks are confirmed, Bracket is final and cannot be changed

7) During the week spreads may vary on games. Spread is as is once picks are confirmed regardless of the spread at time of game. W will be based on spread at time of pick confirm vs game result. Users may have the same game and different spread based on time and date of entry.

8) If a user has both spread and over/under in same game as a pick, for Tie Breaker purposes the total points will be calculated twice.

9) Cut Off Time is 12:45 October 7, 2018


*Tie-Breaker is User prediction of total points scored in User’s games selected.
