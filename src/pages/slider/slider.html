What is a Challenge?

A Challenge is picking 3 or more games and competing your picks against other players for real money.

skip

Types of Challenges

public

Quickly enter a Challenge by letting the app pair you against one or more challengers

private

Allows you to quickly Challenge a friend from your network

custom

Allows you to set your own Challenge options and invite friends from your network

 skip

How do Points work?

There are no point spreads with our Challenges. You just pick winners. Each team is assigned a SH point value. Underdogs are worth more points than favorites. When your team wins, you receive their Value Points.
